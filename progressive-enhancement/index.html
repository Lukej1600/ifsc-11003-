Progressive enhancement is the process of adding features for those with disabilities, or older generations that cant understand anything. It can also be a way for those without access to high end computers, or newer smartphones, to access and use the website as anyone else would. I think as technology rapidly evolves and many new browsers are created almost daily, that progressive enhancement is needed for all the diversity in both users and the technology a user has. If it werent for progressive enhancement, many websites would be unusable for elderly, those with disabilities, and people with older or newer devices.
